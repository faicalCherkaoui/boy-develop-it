BOY DEVELOP IT

A propos

Life lessons are strewn across the Internet these days and it's hard to find ones that you will actually listen to and ones that can actually encourage you to change your life. I came across Admiral William H. McCraven's 2014 University of Texas commencement speech recently that did just that for me. He offers key lessons on how to change your life and maybe even the world to the graduating class of 2014. I don't know anything about SEAL training but I've tried my best to relay. the Admiral's stories here While his speech is 6 years old, the lessons he teaches will last a lifetime if we take them to heart.
